About Me

Currently, I am an assistant professor in OMEGA Lab, Xidian University, working with Prof. Maoguo Gong. Prior to that, I worked as a Researcher in Meituan from 2021 to 2023. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the ZERO Lab, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University in 2021, advised by Prof. Zhouchen Lin.

Research Interest

My research interests include sparse/low-rank theory, recommendation and reinforcement learning.
